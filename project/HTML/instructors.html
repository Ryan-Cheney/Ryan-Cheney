Meet Your Coaches

Ryan Cheney

To me when I was growing up, "living the dream" looked like coaching tennis all I could, and now I am living that dream! I have grown to love tennis so much and have had success playing it primarily because I had passionate and energetic coaches all throughout my junior training who would go out of their way to help me. With VIVA Tennis I hope to bring that same passion and energy to this wonderful sport to help the next generation of tennis players!

  • Ridgeline Head Coach - 3 Years
  • Former no. 1 singles
  • State semi-finalist

Eric Ellis

Since the first time I picked up a racket as a child, tennis has had my heart. For as long as I can remember my goals and aspirations have been tied up in the sport. I can't wait for the opportunity to help others come to love tennis as I have. In my experience as a competitive high school athlete and as a coach I have learned the importance of enjoying every minute spent on court. I look forward to bringing that excitement and love for the sport to VIVA Tennis.

  • Ridgeline Assistant Coach
  • Former no. 1 singles
  • State semi-finalist

Dillon Johnson

I have played tennis practically my whole life, and have worked hard to become the player that I am right now. I have had many great coaches that have helped me to become what I am today, but none of them have been as important as my mom. She has always been there to hit with me and coach me. She has also been there to encourage me to take every opportunity to improve. That is what a good coach can do and I hope to help other people accomplish their goals with VIVA Tennis.

  • Region Champion
  • Current no. 1 singles
  • State finalist
